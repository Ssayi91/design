About Our Company

Creating inspiring spaces and communities across Kenya through innovative design and sustainable development.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Property & Design Kenya began with a vision to transform Kenya's real estate landscape through innovative design and sustainable development. What started as a small team of passionate architects and real estate professionals has grown into a leading company with projects across East Africa.

Our journey began with a single residential project in Nairobi. Today, we've completed over 200 projects including affordable housing communities, luxury residences, commercial spaces, and interior design transformations.

At our core, we believe that well-designed spaces can transform lives and communities. We're proud to contribute to Kenya's development while preserving our rich cultural heritage through thoughtful design.
