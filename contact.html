Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

How soon can you start a new interior design project?

We typically begin new projects within 2-3 weeks of contract signing. The exact timeline depends on the project scope and our current workload. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a clear timeline for your specific project.

What areas do you serve for real estate services?

We serve all major urban areas in Kenya including Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and Eldoret. For interior design services, we work nationwide. Our affordable housing projects are currently focused in Nairobi, Kiambu, and Machakos counties.

Do you offer payment plans for your properties?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for our residential properties. Options include our popular 10-15-75 plan (10% deposit, 15% during construction, 75% on completion) and mortgage partnerships with leading Kenyan banks. Contact us to discuss the best option for your situation.

Can I see your previous interior design projects?

Absolutely! Visit our Portfolio page to see a selection of our completed interior design projects. We can also arrange a visit to our showroom or schedule a consultation to share more examples relevant to your specific project.

How does the affordable housing initiative work?

Our affordable housing program offers quality 2-3 bedroom homes at accessible price points. We provide flexible payment plans and work with government housing programs where applicable. Our developments include community amenities and are built using sustainable, cost-effective construction methods.

Do you provide services for Kenyans in the diaspora?

Yes, we have a dedicated diaspora program. We offer virtual consultations, property viewing via video call, and special payment plans. Our team can manage the entire process remotely, including property management after purchase.
