Featured Project

An in-depth look at one of our most transformative projects.

Nairobi Corporate Office Transformation

CBD, Nairobi

Interior Design Commercial

Client

Leading Fintech Company

Timeline

6 Months (2022)

Size

15,000 sq. ft.

Project Overview

We transformed a traditional office space into a modern, collaborative work environment that reflects Kenya's innovative spirit. The design incorporates local materials, Kenyan artwork, and sustainable design principles.

Design Highlights

  • Open-plan workspace with flexible collaboration zones
  • Integration of Kenyan cultural elements through art and textiles
  • Energy-efficient lighting and climate control systems
  • Biophilic design with indoor plants and natural materials
  • Custom furniture crafted by local artisans

Impact

The new workspace has increased employee satisfaction by 40% and reduced energy consumption by 30%. The client reported improved collaboration and creativity among teams.
